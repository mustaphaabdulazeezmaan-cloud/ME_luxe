About Us

Every great story begins with a dream and Me_Luxe was born from one. A dream to create a world where fashion speaks the language of confidence, culture, and individuality.

At Me_Luxe, we believe every woman carries her own light and our purpose is to help her let it shine. We design not just clothes, but expressions of identity. Each creation is a reflection of the modern woman graceful, bold, ambitious, and free-spirited.

Me_Luxe is more than a fashion brand; it’s a movement. A celebration of womanhood, creativity, and timeless elegance. Our collections tell different stories, yet they all share one heartbeat: the beauty of self-expression.

An_Nisa The Essence of Grace

An_Nisa, meaning “The Women” in Arabic, is our ode to elegance and modesty. Inspired by timeless femininity, this collection blends cultural heritage with modern sophistication from flowing abayas to stylish bubas and feminine silhouettes that speak of quiet strength. Every stitch, every fold, is designed to remind women that grace is not silent it’s powerful, radiant, and deeply inspiring.

Bags by Me Carry Your Story

Every woman carries more than what fits in a bag she carries dreams, strength, and ambition. Bags by Me was born from this understanding. Our handcrafted pieces blend functionality with luxury, turning everyday essentials into timeless treasures. From minimalist classics to statement designs, each bag is crafted to move with her from daily hustle to moments of triumph reminding her that she is enough, and that her story is worth carrying beautifully.

Urban Me The Spirit of the City

The Urban Me collection captures the pulse of modern life raw, bold, and endlessly creative. It’s where fashion meets freedom. Inspired by street culture and city energy, Urban Me is designed for the confident woman who dares to stand out. With edgy silhouettes, vibrant tones, and expressive cuts, it redefines casual wear as a form of empowerment. Urban Me is not just fashion; it’s attitude, rhythm, and movement.

At Me_Luxe, we don’t just design clothes we design confidence. We don’t just follow trends we create timeless experiences. Our passion is to empower women to feel strong, elegant, and authentically themselves.

Because when you wear Me_Luxe, you’re not just wearing a brand. You’re telling your story one outfit, one moment, one dream at a time.
